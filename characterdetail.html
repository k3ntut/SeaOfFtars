Zale

Solar Bladedancer

Born on the Summer Solstice, Zale is optimistic and bold. He wields a sunblade, through which he can absorb Eclipse Orbs whenever his attacks need an extra kick.


As a blade-dancer, he walks the path of the Guardian God Solen. The Elder Mist foretold that, in order to find hish light and achieve his full potential, he will first need to "stare at the night inside of him".
